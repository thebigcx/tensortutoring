About our program

Passionate tutors

The biggest factor in a student's engagement is the passion with which their tutor delivers material. Our tutors have fostered a love of learning and are excited to inspire students with new ideas.

An integrated learning process

We believe that students benefit most from practice problems and individualised feedback. That is why we assign weekly quizzes to students and provide 24/7 assistance, services that are often missed within the realm of HSC tutoring.

Accelerated learning

The more times you see something, the slower you will forget it. That is why we deliver content a couple weeks in advance to the student learning it at school, ensuring we overcome the forgetting curve.
